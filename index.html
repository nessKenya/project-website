Especially For Youth Africa

The objective of EFY is to strengthen youth in their commitment to live the gospel of Jesus Christ by providing inspiring and edifying learning opportunities and wholesome social experiences. In the mid-1970s, Ron Hills and other BYU employees developed a program to “create an Education Week for teenagers where youth could interact with other young people from various places in the country, rather than just with those from their own ward or stake.” That program was called Especially for Youth (EFY).

Youth Conferences

What is FSY? For the Strength of Youth (FSY) conferences are large conferences for youth held to help you apply the gospel in all aspects of your life. At FSY, you will participate in five days of devotionals, classes, and activities designed to build and strengthen your faith in Jesus Christ as you grow spiritually, socially, physically, and intellectually.

FSY Conferences Postponed Until 2021 Due to the ongoing impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, it has become necessary to postpone For the Strength of Youth (FSY) conferences scheduled to be held in the U.S. and Canada in 2020 until 2021. Youth from stakes invited in 2020 who still meet the age requirements will be invited to attend FSY in 2021 and registration details will be communicated at a later date.
